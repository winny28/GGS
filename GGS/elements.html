About Us

Founders

Hi Neighbors, our names are Jack and David, proud partners and owners of Greenwich Garden Service LLC. Both born in and raised in Greenwich, We take pride in the preservation of the aesthetic in all of our clients property. We have been servicing Greenwich and the surrounding areas since 2013. Our extensive list of services guarantee our commitment of any of your landscape needs. We service Greenwich, Stamford, Armonk and Bedford for plowing and weekly maintenance, and all surrounding areas for all other services. Our ambition for perfection is what motivates us to provide you with the quality and care your property deserves.
